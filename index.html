Help Center

Getting Started
  1. Create income and expense categories in Settings.
  2. Add your first transactions from Dashboard or Transactions.
  3. Create budgets to track monthly spending limits.
  4. Add bills to stay on top of due dates.
  5. Review Reports for insights and trends.
Quick Tips
  • Use Notes to add context to each transaction.
  • Turn on notifications for bill reminders.
  • Switch themes anytime from Settings.
  • Data is stored locally for offline-first use.
FAQ
