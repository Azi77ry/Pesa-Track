## ğŸ“± First-Time User Tutorial

### Adding Your First Transaction

1. **Click "Add Income" or "Add Expense"** (top-right corner)
2. **Fill in the details:**
   - Amount: Enter the transaction amount
   - Category: Select from dropdown (e.g., Salary, Groceries)
   - Date: Choose transaction date
   - Note: Optional description
3. **Click "Save"**

âœ¨ Your transaction is now saved and visible on the dashboard!

### Creating Your First Budget

1. **Navigate to "Budgets"** (top navigation or bottom mobile menu)
2. **Click "Add Budget"**
3. **Set your budget:**
   - Category: Choose expense category (e.g., Groceries)
   - Amount: Monthly budget limit
   - Period: Monthly, Quarterly, or Yearly
4. **Click "Save"**

You'll now see:
- Current spending
- Budget progress bar
- Remaining amount
- Alerts when you reach 80% or 100%

### Adding Your First Bill

1. **Go to "Bills & Payments"**
2. **Click "Add Bill"**
3. **Enter bill details:**
   - Name: Bill description (e.g., "Electric Bill")
   - Amount: Bill amount
   - Due Date: When payment is due
   - Recurring: One-time, Monthly, Quarterly, or Yearly
   - Category: Select relevant category
4. **Click "Save"**

**When you pay a bill:**
- Click "Pay" button
- Bill marked as paid
- Expense automatically created
- If recurring, next bill auto-generated

### Viewing Your Financial Reports

1. **Click "Reports & Analytics"**
2. **View three powerful charts:**
   - **Pie Chart**: Expenses by category
   - **Line Chart**: Monthly spending trends (last 12 months)
   - **Bar Chart**: Income vs Expenses comparison (last 6 months)
